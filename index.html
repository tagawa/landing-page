GHOST is a Proof of Stake privacy coin to help make you nothing but a "ghost" when transacting online!

Whitepaper Litepaper

Privacy

GHOST transactions use a state of the art escrow pool to shield and erase the history of transactions. GHOST transactions are verified using zero-knowledge proofs.

Decentralized

GHOST is a proof-of-stake network controlled by GHOST token holders and users. GHOST has no central company or owner, and is ran and maintained by the community.

Faster Transaction Times / Low Fees

With GHOST, transactions are processed on chain in under 60 seconds with just a fraction of a penny paid in transaction fees.

Masternodes

Help secure the GHOST network and earn a share of the transaction fees using the staking and masternode features for GHOST.

How to get GHOST rewards

1. Staking

Staking GHOST allows you to share in the fees genereated by the GHOST blockchain. Staking has no minimum coin requirements.

2. Run a masternode

Running a GHOST masternode requires 20,000 GHOST and is the best way to share in the fees generated by the GHOST blockchain. You can run as many masternodes as you would like.

How is GHOST created and distributed?


In the beginning, 6 new GHOST tokens are minted each block.

3 GHOST are split between masternodes
2 GHOST are split between stakers
1 GHOST goes into a pool used for future GHOST development and growth

Total supply : 55,000,000
